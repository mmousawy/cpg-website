We've been shipping a lot of updates lately and wanted to give you a quick rundown of what's new. Whether you've been on the site daily or haven't logged in for a bit, there's plenty to check out.

Photo challenges

We've added themed photo challenges where you can submit your best work around a creative prompt. Accepted photos get featured in a challenge gallery and earn a badge on your profile. Check the Challenges page to see what's running.

Shared albums

You can now create collaborative albums and invite other members to contribute. Everyone gets credited for their photos, and you control whether the album is open or invite-only.

Event albums

Every event now gets its own photo album automatically. After attending a meetup, you can upload your shots directly to the event page so everyone can relive it together.

Photo management

You can now delete your photos directly from the detail page, and paginated lists remember your scroll position when you navigate back.

Help and FAQ

We added a full Help page covering everything from getting started to managing your account. Link's in the footer.
