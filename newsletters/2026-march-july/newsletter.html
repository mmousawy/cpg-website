Hey there!

We haven't been sitting still these past couple of months: new features, a refreshed profile experience, and plenty of polish along the way. Here's a quick overview of what’s changed since March.

Scene events

A new Scene section is live on the platform: a community-curated guide to photography happenings beyond our own meetups. Browse exhibitions, photowalks, talks, workshops, festivals, and more. Mark interest, see who else is going, and join the conversation in comments. You can also submit your own events to share with the community.

Profile banners

You can now add a custom banner to your profile. Upload and crop an image from Account settings and it shows at the top of your profile - a nice way to set the vibe of your page.

Feedback

You can now send feedback, bug reports, and feature requests directly from the site. Pick a subject, write your message, and attach up to three screenshots if that helps explain things. Look for the feedback option in the footer.

Account deletion

Members can now delete their own account from Account settings. Your content is hidden right away and you won't be able to log back in. After 30 days, everything is permanently removed. You'll receive a confirmation email with the deletion date in case you change your mind.

Onboarding improvements

New members get a friendlier first-run experience - a warmer welcome screen and clearer "(optional)" labels on profile fields so you know what's required.

Other improvements

  • Cleaner date formatting across the site
  • Better mobile experience and consistent styling
  • Unsaved-changes protection when closing forms and modals
  • Help page list formatting improvements

We'd love to hear what you think - your feedback shapes what we build next. Log in, explore what's new, and if you know a photographer who'd enjoy this community, share the link. The more creative eyes, the better it gets.

Happy shooting!
